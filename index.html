How HerHands Works

Our simple three-step process makes it easy for women to start selling and for customers to support them.

1

Create Your Seller Profile

Sign up for free and set up your personalized seller profile. Tell your story and showcase your craft.

2

List Your Products

Upload photos, add descriptions, and set prices for your handmade or home-made products.

3

Start Selling & Earning

Reach customers, manage orders, and receive secure payments directly to your account.
